Far East & Asia

Sail through turquoise waters on an exotic cruise of Southeast Asia. Not only is this a culturally fascinating region, its staggering natural beauty is impossible to forget. Many cruise itineraries start in Bangkok or Singapore, both must-see destinations, and give you a taste of countries including Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam and Thailand’s famously idyllic beaches. It’s a great opportunity to extend your cruise with book-end hotel stays too.
